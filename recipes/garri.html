Drinking Garri Recipe

Hmmm Garri!

Garri is another classic dish widely popular in a lot African household, regardless of region or ethnicity.

Itâ€™s made of peeled and ground cassava.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup white garri
  • 1 tbsp powdered milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp grated coconut
  • Small quantity groundnut(peanut)

Steps for a drinking Garri

Like traditional cereal, it's super easy to make.

  1. Put some Garri in a bowl, pour in some water way above the level of the Garri
  2. Sieve our the floating particles.
  3. Add more water, some ice cubes, sugar / sweetener, groundnuts and enjoy
